Our Programs


ESL

This program will teach you how to read, write, speak and listen to English. As both children and adult students learn English, the benefit of ESL is that they can communicate with native-speaking friends and family within the community while reaching out to people who communicate only in English. These ELLs can bridge the gap between their families and the rest of the world. Classes for both kids and adults.
